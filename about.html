Samiha Shoshi is a Web Designer and Developer, who is currently a student at Drexel University in the User Experience and Interactions Design program. She is the first of three daughters to attend college in her family. In her definition, family is anyone that loves and supports you and your lifelong goals. She believes that creating a family-like bond with clients is important. This is because as the designer, she wants to see the companies she works with flourish and achieve all their business goals.

Her background in human factors engineering, psychology, user interface, and digital design inform her user-friendly but aesthetically pleasing design approach. Samiha is fueled by her passion for learning new design technics and helping people with their website needs. She considers herself to be a student forever, as she is eager to learn new languages. Plus, there is always something new to be learned, even after mastering the skill.
