Scripting I : Zodiac Sign Site

During this project, I was able to apply my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. First, I began this process by creating a flowchart to map certain characters with specific zodiac signs. Next, I created a low-fidelity wireframe of the website. After organizing all my content, I began to code the site to look like my design and then began working on writing the Javascript required for this site to work properly. It was very fascinating to see and learn all the animations and additional functions one can apply to their site just with the use of Javascript.
